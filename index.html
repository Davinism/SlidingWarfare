SLIDING WARFARE

  • Objective:
  • Shoot all enemy shooters or shoot the enemy base first before the enemy does!
  • Rules:
  • Use keyboard to select and move and fire shooters.
  • Only allowed 3 actions per turn.
  • Translation (W, A, S, D, and Arrow Keys) and Shooting count as 1 action each.
  • All other moves(rotation, toggling shooters) does not take up an action.

Press Enter to play

  • Move Up: W or Up Arrow
  • Move Left: A or Left Arrow
  • Move Down: S or Down Arrow
  • Move Right: D or Right Arrow
  • Rotate Clockwise: E
  • Rotate Counter-clockwise: Q
  • Toggle Selections: R
  • Fire: Space
